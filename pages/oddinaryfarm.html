Overview

Arcade Farming Gameplay

The arcade farming system offers an engaging experience where players can expand their settlements and interact with the environment in a way that respects their time.

Network Synchronization

Networked multiplayer gameplay, powered by Unity's Netcode for GameObjects, allows players to collaborate on managing resources, cultivating crops, raising livestock, and exploring the world together.

Procedural Generation

The ever-changing world created through procedural algorithms using Perlin noise combined with natural geographical phenomena rules, resulting in a unique, Earth-like environment.
