Hell's Kitchen

Restaurant Management Game

Hell's Kitchen is a restaurant management game about a totured soul in hell prepetually serving its citizen in a fast paced and challenging enviroment.

Role Lead Programmer
Platform Window, Mobile, Web
Engine Unity
Tool Used Asprite, Unity Version Control, Trello
Team Size 4

Overview
    The early concept of Hell's Kitchen originated from Ludum Dare 51 Game Jam in 2022. The game was in production for 3 days before hosted on itch.io. It recieved many reviews, including a constructive feedback from the popular streamer. The team has then improved on the original concept and planned to release the game sometime this year - early next year
Development Process
  • Challenge:
    • Large variance in team members experience
    • Time constraint (72 hours for the original build)
  • Skill leanred:
    • Scope management
    • Establish onboarding process
  • Innovation:
    • GameObject Pool Manager
    • Reactive UI
    • Touch support for multiple devices and platform
