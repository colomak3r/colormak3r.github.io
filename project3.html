Role Lead Programmer
Platform Window, Mobile, Web
Engine Unity
Tool Used Asprite, Unity Version Control, Trello
Team Size Solo

Overview
    Oddinary farm as its core is a sandbox game, but it's also a sandbox for me to try out new technology and programming techniques. It's different than traditional game where I have to deliver a feature on a strict deadline. Here in Oddinary farm, there are no deadline. A feature can take as long as it needed to materialize.
