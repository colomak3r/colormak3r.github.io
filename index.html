Hello, again! I'm Khoa.

I’m currently in my final year at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF), majoring in computer science with a passion for video game development.

I love diving into code, whether it’s crafting gameplay mechanics in Unity, experimenting with procedural generation, or optimizing performance for smoother experiences. I’m fluent in languages like C#, C++, and x86 Assembly, and I’ve had a blast working with tools like Unity, Godot, Photoshop, and Git to bring my creative ideas to life.

Beyond the technical side, I’m all about collaboration and teamwork. As the treasurer of the CSUF Video Game Development Club, I’ve had the chance to connect with fellow enthusiasts, plan events, and keep things running smoothly. I’ve also worked as a Supplemental Instruction Leader, helping my peers tackle tricky subjects like Computer Architecture and x86 Assembly Programming.

When I’m not coding or brainstorming my next game idea, you’ll probably find me skateboarding, rolling sushi, solving Rubik cubes, and gaming with friends. I’m always up for new adventures and challenges, and I can’t wait to see where this journey takes me next!
